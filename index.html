ciao, I'm Claire Szeto

...and design is how I interact with the world.

My work is the development of my environment and myself as the catalyst for transformation of that environment.
I am humbled and guided by the process and strive to wrap discovery in gorgeous visual design.

My working style is collaborative, and I use my leadership powers to guide decision and move teams forward.

Presently, re-tooled in UX and UI, I'm excited to leverage my previous design lives into helping users achieve optimal results.
